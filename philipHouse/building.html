the building

Philip House is a classic prewar condominium residence reimagined for modern life. While retaining the authenticity of an Upper East Side home, its interiors have been entirely rebuilt. A rare find in Carnegie Hill, Philip House captures the grace of tradition and the casual ease of a contemporary lifestyle.
